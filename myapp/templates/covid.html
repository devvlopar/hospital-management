COVID-19

Contact your local health department for the most appropriate information for your area.

To prevent the spread of COVID-19:

  • Maintain a safe distance (at least 1 meter) from others, even if they are not sick.
  • Wear a mask in public places, especially inside buildings or when physical distancing is not possible.
  • Choose open, well-ventilated spaces instead of closed ones. If inside a building, open the windows.
  • Wash hands frequently. To wash hands, use soap and water or an alcohol hand rub.
  • Get vaccinated when it's your turn. Follow local instructions on vaccination.
  • Cover your nose and mouth with elbow or handkerchief when coughing or sneezing.
  • If you are feeling unwell, stay at home.

If you have fever, cough and difficulty in breathing, go to the doctor. Contact your healthcare provider well in advance, so that they will tell you where to go for treatment. It protects you and prevents the spread of viruses and other infections.
